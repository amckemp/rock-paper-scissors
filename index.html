This game is played in the console.

How to Play:

  1. Right click anywhere on the page, then click 'Inspect'
  2. At the top of the new window that opened, click 'Console', then type 'game()' and push 'enter' on your keyboard
  3. The game will prompt you to choose either rock, paper, or scissors.
  4. Choose one, then play away!
